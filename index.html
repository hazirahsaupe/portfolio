About Me

Final Year Statistics Student

Final Year Statistics student at Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) with experience in R programming, data analysis, statistical modelling, data visualization and research projects...

Final Year Statistics student at Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) with a strong foundation in statistical analysis, data management, and research methodologies. Experienced in using R programming for data cleaning, visualization, regression analysis, and statistical modelling.


Throughout academic projects, I have worked with real-world datasets to identify significant variables, interpret analytical results, and transform complex data into meaningful insights for decision-making.


My Final Year Project focuses on analysing the determinants of life expectancy in Malaysia, Singapore, and Thailand using the ARDL approach. This project strengthened my skills in econometric modelling, hypothesis testing, data interpretation, and report writing.


In addition, I have experience developing database systems using Oracle, building applications using C++, and designing digital content using Canva. I am also actively involved in leadership and volunteer activities which have enhanced my communication, teamwork, and problem-solving abilities.


Passionate about transforming data into meaningful insights, I am eager to apply my analytical skills in real-world environments and gain valuable industry experience through internship opportunities in data analytics, business intelligence, and related fields.
